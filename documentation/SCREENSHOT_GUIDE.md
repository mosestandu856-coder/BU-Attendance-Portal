## How to Take Screenshots

**On Windows:**
- Press `Windows + Shift + S` to open Snipping Tool
- Select the area you want to capture
- Save as PNG

**On Phone:**
- Press Power + Volume Down simultaneously
- Or use the screenshot gesture for your phone model

---

## Where to Save Screenshots

Save all screenshots in:
```
documentation/screenshots/
```

Name them clearly, e.g.:
- `01-home-page.png`
- `02-login-page.png`
- `03-admin-dashboard.png`
- etc.
